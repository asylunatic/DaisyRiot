All is set up. Get ready to play around!
		
Press:
Arrow keys to move around the scene
P to make a screenshot
B to calculate a form factor between two selected patches
F to find a triangle by id
R to toggle Radiosity vs normal color drawing (take note that initially the light passes have not yet been calculated!)
L to render one light pass
C to converge the radiosity calculations
X to clear the radiosity calculations (so you can calculate the light passes again!)
