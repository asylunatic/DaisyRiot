

All is set up. Get ready to play around!
	
Left click and drag to rotate view.	
Right click on a triangle to select it, click on a second triangle to calculate if they can see each other.
Press arrow keys to move around the scene
Press P to make a screenshot
Press B to calculate a form factor between two selected patches
Press F to find a triangle by id
Press R to toggle Radiosity vs normal color drawing
Press X to clear the radiosity calculations (so you can calculate the light passes again!)
Press L to calculate one light pass
Press C to converge the radiosity calculations
